## Origin

This promotion was designed using **M4ROI agent research** — an AI-assisted marketing methodology that analyzes customer reviews, competitor gaps, and pricing signals to identify the highest-leverage promotional opportunity for a business. For Casa Lupe, the agent identified dead-hour foot traffic (3–6 PM) and margarita/fajita price sensitivity as the clearest conversion opportunity.

---

## What This Page Tests

Rather than a branding campaign, this is a **demand hypothesis test** run through a single landing page + Google Ads. The core question:

> *Can a clear, time-bound discount on margaritas and chicken fajitas convert cold local search traffic into first-time dine-in customers during otherwise low-revenue afternoon hours?*

### The 5 Hypotheses

| # | Hypothesis | Signal to Watch |
|---|---|---|
| H1 | A visible % discount is enough to motivate a nearby customer to choose Casa Lupe | Form submission rate (target: >3% of clicks) |
| H2 | Discounted 3–6 PM covers generate more margin than empty seats | Incremental covers per week vs. pre-campaign baseline |
| H3 | Google Ads local search reaches high-intent customers in 94041/94040 | Ad CTR (benchmark: 4–6% for restaurant local) |
| H4 | The Google Form is a CRM seed — every submission is a named, emailed lead | Form submissions vs. actual in-store redemptions |
| H5 | Showing crossed-out prices (~~$15~~ → $9) outperforms flat "special price" framing | Qualitative now; A/B testable in round 2 |
